Natural Solutions for Stomach Aches

Stomach aches can arise from a variety of issues, ranging from indigestion to overeating or even more serious medical conditions. While it's important to consult a doctor for persistent or severe pain, there are numerous natural remedies that can provide relief for mild to moderate discomfort.

Causes

Natural Treatments

  • Indigestion: Difficulty in digesting food, leading to discomfort.
  • Overeating: Consuming more food than the stomach can comfortably hold.
  • Gas: Build-up of gas in the stomach and intestines.
  • Gastric Infection: Caused by bacteria or viruses.
  • Ulcers: Sores on the lining of the stomach or small intestine.
  • Other Causes: Menstrual cramps, medications, hernia, etc.
  • Warm Compress: Applying warmth to the stomach area can help relax muscles and alleviate pain.
  • Ginger: Known for its anti-inflammatory properties, it can help with indigestion and nausea.
  • Peppermint Tea: Can soothe the stomach and ease gas pains.
  • Chamomile Tea: Has anti-inflammatory properties and can help with indigestion.
  • BRAT Diet: Bananas, Rice, Applesauce, and Toast can be gentle on the stomach.
  • Avoid Spicy/Fried Foods: Such foods can irritate the stomach further.
