Common Causes of Stomach Aches:

  • Indigestion: Discomfort in your upper belly or abdomen.
  • Gas: Pain, bloating, and belching caused by trapped gas.
  • Constipation: Less frequent bowel movements with hard, dry stools.
  • Gastritis: Inflammation of the stomach lining.
