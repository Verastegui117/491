Common Signs of Lethargy:

  • Constant Fatigue: A persistent feeling of tiredness or lack of energy.
  • Mental Exhaustion: Difficulty in concentrating or staying focused.
  • Muscle Weakness: Reduced strength or endurance in muscles.
  • Low Motivation: Lack of enthusiasm or drive for activities.
